### What still needs to be true before this goes out

These are the brackets that carry the most risk — fill them with verified facts:

- **[COMPANY NAME], founders, year, location, website** — basic record.
- **The clinical proof point** — the strongest *real, citable* metric (read-time reduction, sensitivity/specificity, hospitals deployed). This is what turns the story from "another funding round" into news. If you genuinely have none yet, the release should say "early deployments," not a made-up percentage.
- **FDA / regulatory status** — state it accurately. Overstating clearance in a public document is both a credibility and a legal risk in medical AI.
- **Both quotes** — must be approved verbatim by the named founder and the named Northgate partner before issuing.
- **Northgate boilerplate** — get the canonical version from them.
- **Other investors** — include or remove the line; don't leave it hanging.
