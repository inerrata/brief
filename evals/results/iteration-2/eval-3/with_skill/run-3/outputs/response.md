Here are 3 subject line options, each with a distinct angle:

---

**1. Urgency**
`48 hours. Our best coffee deal of the year.`

*Preview text:* [X]% off — ends Sunday at midnight.

---

**2. Curiosity**
`We almost didn't send this email`

*Preview text:* But it's Black Friday, and you deserve a good deal on great coffee.

---

**3. Specificity/direct**
`Black Friday: [X]% off your next 3 bags`

*Preview text:* Stock up on your favourites — deal valid this weekend only.

---

**When to use each:**
- **Option 1** — if you want urgency to drive opens and you have a genuine deadline.
- **Option 2** — if your brand has personality and your list is warm; the intrigue earns the open.
- **Option 3** — if your audience responds to clear, no-nonsense offers and you want no ambiguity.

*Fill in the actual discount figure — specific numbers consistently outperform vague language like "huge savings."*
