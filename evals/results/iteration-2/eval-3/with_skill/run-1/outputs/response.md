Here are 3 subject line options, each taking a distinct angle:

---

**1. Urgency/scarcity**
`Your Black Friday coffee deal expires tonight`

*Preview text:* Up to [X]% off your next 3 months — clock's ticking.

---

**2. Curiosity/pattern interrupt**
`We don't do Black Friday. (Except this year.)`

*Preview text:* A one-time offer from us, for you — valid this weekend only.

---

**3. Direct benefit**
`Black Friday: [X]% off every bag, all weekend`

*Preview text:* Stock up on your favourites before the deal ends Sunday.

---

**Which to use:** Option 1 works if you have a hard deadline to enforce. Option 2 works if your brand doesn't typically run sales and you want to make the email feel special. Option 3 works if your audience is deal-focused and you just want to be clear and direct.

*Note: Replace [X]% with your actual discount — a specific number always outperforms a vague promise like "big savings."*
