Here are 3 subject line options, each taking a genuinely different angle:

---

**1. Direct benefit**
`Black Friday: [X]% off every subscription`

*Preview text:* Our biggest discount of the year — this weekend only.

---

**2. Social proof / warmth**
`Your fellow coffee lovers are stocking up. Here's why.`

*Preview text:* Our Black Friday deal is live — and it's the one our subscribers ask for every year.

---

**3. Pattern interrupt / personality**
`Black Friday smells like freshly ground coffee`

*Preview text:* (And savings. Mostly savings. Up to [X]% off this weekend.)

---

**Notes:**
- Option 1 is the safest choice for a deal-focused list — clear and specific.
- Option 2 works well if your brand leans on community and loyalty.
- Option 3 fits a brand with a playful, sensory voice — it earns a smile before the click.

*Replace [X]% with the actual figure. A real number is always stronger than "big savings" or "exclusive deal."*
