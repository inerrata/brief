# Thank-You Note to Grandma

Here's a warm note you can use or adapt:

---

Dear Grandma,

Thank you so much for the birthday sweater — it's such a thoughtful gift. It's cozy and beautiful, and I know I'm going to wear it all the time. You always know exactly what I'll love.

It means a lot to feel so remembered and cared for on my birthday. Thank you for everything you do.

With all my love,
[Your name]

---

Feel free to personalize it — add a specific detail about the sweater, mention something you're looking forward to doing while wearing it, or adjust the sign-off to match how you usually address her.
