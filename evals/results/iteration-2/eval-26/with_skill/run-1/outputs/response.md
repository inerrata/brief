# Thank-You Note to Grandma

Here's a warm, genuine note you can send as-is or adjust to fit your voice:

---

Dear Grandma,

Thank you so much for the birthday sweater — it's absolutely lovely. I can already tell it's going to be one of those pieces I reach for again and again when I want to feel cozy. The color is perfect.

It means a lot that you thought of me and took the time to choose something so thoughtful. I'm lucky to have you.

With love,
[Your name]

---

If you want a slightly more casual or more formal version, just let me know and I can adjust the tone.
