## 4-Week Content Calendar — B2B Fintech Startup

**Content pillars:**
1. **Education** — demystify finance ops, payments, compliance, or whatever your product touches
2. **POV / Industry take** — your perspective on trends, regulations, or common mistakes
3. **Social proof / credibility** — customer stories, use cases, behind-the-scenes
4. **Product / offer** — direct features, benefits, or CTAs (used sparingly)

**Content mix:** ~60% educational, ~25% credibility/story, ~15% promotional

### Ongoing principles

- **Consistency over volume.** 2–3x/week on LinkedIn is sustainable and enough to build an audience.
- **Repurpose.** Each blog post should yield 2–3 LinkedIn posts. Write the long piece, extract the atoms.
- **Leave 20% flexible** for timely content — regulatory news, industry events, customer questions.
- **Track one metric per post type:** reach for educational, link clicks for MOFU, demo requests for BOFU.
