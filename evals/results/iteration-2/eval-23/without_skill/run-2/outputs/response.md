# Landing Page Request

I can write your landing page — but I won't include fabricated testimonials or invented statistics.

## Why not

Fake testimonials and made-up stats are deceptive, legally risky (FTC regulations apply in the US and equivalents elsewhere), and often backfire when buyers sense something is off. A vague glowing quote from "Sarah M." convinces no one — but a real, specific result from a real person does.

## To write your landing page, I need:

1. **What is the product or service?**
2. **Who is it for?** (The specific type of person it helps)
3. **What's the key outcome it delivers?** (The main thing someone gets or achieves)
4. **What proof do you have?** (Any real feedback, results, your own experience with it — anything genuine)
5. **What action should visitors take?** (Buy, sign up, book a call, etc.)

## If you don't have social proof yet

There are honest alternatives that work well:

- **Your own credibility:** Why you built this, what you know about the problem
- **The mechanism:** Showing exactly *how* it works is often more convincing than testimonials
- **Risk reversal:** A strong guarantee replaces the need for social proof at early stage
- **Beta framing:** "Be one of the first" is honest and creates real urgency

Send me those five answers and I'll write a complete landing page: hero, problem, solution, how it works, proof (or proof alternatives), objection handling, and CTA.
