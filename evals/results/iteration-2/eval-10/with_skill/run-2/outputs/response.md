## Audience

**Who:** Freelance graphic, UX, and brand designers — solo operators billing clients by the hour or project, typically working across 3–8 active clients at once.

**What they believe NOW:** Tracking time is annoying admin overhead; they either don't do it, wing it in a spreadsheet, or use a clunky tool that doesn't fit their workflow.

**What we want them to believe AFTER:** Accurate time tracking takes 2 minutes a day, surfaces exactly which clients and project types are worth their time, and makes invoicing frictionless — so they stop leaving money on the table.

**Core pain:** Freelance designers consistently underestimate the time they spend on projects. They quote flat rates based on gut feel, eat scope creep silently, and end up earning less per hour than they realize. The problem isn't laziness — it's that tracking feels like more work than it's worth.

**You're probably billing for less than half the time you're actually working.**

## Channels

| Channel | Rationale |

## Timeline (6 Weeks)

| Week | Phase | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| **1** | Pre-launch | Finalize landing page, set up trial tracking, create all creative assets, write email sequence, schedule paid campaign |
| **2** | Launch | Announce across all owned channels; activate paid ads; seed in Reddit/communities |
| **3–4** | Sustain | Weekly email to non-converters; A/B test ad creative; share early user proof |
| **5** | Amplify | Double down on best-performing channel; add urgency angle if needed (e.g., limited onboarding offer) |
| **6** | Close & capture | Final push to trial-started-but-not-converted; debrief on CAC and channel efficiency |

## Budget Tier

- **Lean (under $2K):** Organic + community-seeding dominant; small retargeting budget ($500–$1K)
- **Mid ($2K–$10K):** Paid social (Meta/Instagram) + sponsored newsletter placement + retargeting
- **Scale ($10K+):** Full paid social, design publication ads, potential influencer/design creator partnership
