# Name Ideas for Your Grey, Lazy Cat

Here are some names that fit a grey coat and a sleepy, laid-back personality:

**Personality-forward (lazy/chill):**
- **Noodle** — boneless, relaxed energy
- **Snooze** — self-explanatory
- **Mellow**
- **Pudding** — soft and settles into any shape

**Grey-inspired:**
- **Ash** or **Asher**
- **Misty**
- **Slate**
- **Smoke** or **Smokey**
- **Pebble**

**Dignified names that contrast the laziness (often very fitting):**
- **Duchess** — the title is undermined by her lifestyle
- **Countess**
- **Baroness**

**Classic cozy picks:**
- **Velvet** — grey and soft
- **Dusty**

Top pick: **Ash** for sleek and grey, or **Duchess** if you enjoy the irony of a very lazy noble.
