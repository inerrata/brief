## Search Intent
**Commercial investigation** — the searcher is comparing CRM options before choosing one for their organization. They already know they need a CRM; they want a credible, specific recommendation for the nonprofit context.

**Dominant content format:** Listicle/comparison ("Best X for Y" articles dominate this SERP). Confirm by searching the keyword before writing — match the format Google is already rewarding.

## Differentiation Angle
Most comparison articles in this space are generic listicles with shallow entries. Stand out by: (1) giving honest, specific verdicts by org size and use case rather than hedging every recommendation, and (2) covering nonprofit-specific features (grant tracking, board reporting) that generic CRM reviews ignore.
