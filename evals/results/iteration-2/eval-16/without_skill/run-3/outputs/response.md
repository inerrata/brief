# Win-Back Sequence — Inactive Customers (60+ Days)

**Trigger:** No login for 60 consecutive days
**Goal:** Reactivate customers. After the final email with no response, suppress from marketing sends.

---

## Email 1 — Check-in (Day 60)

**Subject:** Haven't seen you in a while, [First name]
**Preview text:** Wanted to check in.

Hey [First name],

I noticed you haven't logged into [Product] in about two months. Wanted to reach out personally and see if everything's okay.

Did something get in the way? Is there something that wasn't working for you? I'd love to know — just hit reply.

If you've just been busy and want to jump back in: **[Log in here →]**

[Name / Team]

---

## Email 3 — Incentive (Day 72)

**Subject:** A reason to come back, [First name]
**Preview text:** Limited time — expires [date].

Hey [First name],

Still haven't seen you around. I get it — life gets busy.

If you've been thinking about coming back but haven't made the jump, here's a nudge: we're offering [specific incentive — e.g., one free month, an onboarding call, 20% off next renewal] for customers who re-engage in the next 7 days.

**[Claim your offer and log back in →]**

Offer expires [date].

[Name]

---

## Email 4 — Final Notice (Day 79)

**Subject:** Our last note to you, [First name]
**Preview text:** We'll leave you alone after this.

Hey [First name],

This is the last email we'll send about coming back.

If [Product] isn't a fit right now, no hard feelings. You can [cancel/pause/keep your account — whatever applies].

If there's something specific that caused you to step away — a missing feature, a confusing workflow, a pricing issue — I'd really appreciate hearing it. Just reply to this email.

And if you do want to come back: **[Log in →]**

Thanks for trying [Product]. Hope to see you again.

[Name]

---

## After the sequence

- **No engagement:** Remove from marketing sends. Keep in a suppressed segment.
- **Replies with feedback:** Route to support or product team — this is actionable intelligence.
- **Opened but didn't click:** Consider a targeted re-engagement if a major product update happens in the next 6 months.

---

## Timing summary

| Email | Day | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 60 | Human check-in, low pressure |
| 2 | 65 | New value / product updates |
| 3 | 72 | Incentive with real deadline |
| 4 | 79 | Final notice, honest close |
