## ICP: "The ops lead drowning in stockouts and spreadsheets"

**Firmographics**
- Company: SMB–mid-market businesses holding physical inventory — 3PLs, distributors, e-commerce/DTC brands, light manufacturers.
- Size: 1–10 warehouses, ~$5M–$100M revenue, 20–500 employees.
- Tech: outgrowing spreadsheets or a basic WMS bolt-on; already on some ERP/e-comm stack.

**Decision role**
- Champion/user: Operations Manager / Warehouse Manager / Head of Supply Chain.
- Economic buyer: COO or owner at smaller firms.

**Goals (jobs-to-be-done)**
- Know real-time stock levels across locations; stop overselling and stockouts.
- Cut manual counts and reconciliation time; ship accurately.

**Pains**
- Stockouts and oversells losing sales; dead stock tying up cash.
- Spreadsheet chaos, no single source of truth, error-prone counts.

**Buying triggers**
- A costly stockout/oversell incident; peak-season scaling; opening a new location; a failed audit; new ERP migration.

**Where to reach them**
- Supply-chain/ops subreddits and LinkedIn groups, 3PL/e-comm communities, ERP/marketplace app directories, ops-focused newsletters and trade shows.
