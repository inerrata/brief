A value proposition is a load-bearing statement — your homepage, ads, and onboarding all ladder up to it, so a guessed differentiator quietly weakens everything. Three quick questions and I'll make it sharp:

2. **What's the one thing your platform does that the alternatives don't?** Versus YouTube (free), Skillshare/MasterClass, or a teacher's own Teachable site — what's your unfair advantage?
3. **What's the main alternative they'd use instead of you?** That's what your value prop has to beat.
