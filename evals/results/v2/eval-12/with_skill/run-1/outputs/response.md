*Assuming: a farmers-market coffee cart; goal = get walk-by foot traffic to stop and buy. Adjust if wrong.*

A sign has about two seconds to work, so lead with a smell-you-can-read or a single clear offer — not "Coffee."

Two options:

**Option A — sensory hook**
> **Fresh Espresso, Pulled to Order**
> Real beans. Real steam. Two steps away. →

**Option B — offer hook**
> **Market Morning Special**
> Any coffee + [item] for [$X]
> Cash & card • Right here →

Make the biggest word the *benefit or smell*, not your brand name. Add an arrow pointing at the cart, and chalk the day's drink ("Honey Oat Latte") so people know what to order on the spot.
