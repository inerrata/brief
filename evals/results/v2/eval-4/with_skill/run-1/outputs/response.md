## Campaign concept: "Sweat Together"

**Core message (one):** *Working out is easier when a friend's in it with you — bring one, and you both win.*

**Mechanic (double-sided reward):**
- Referrer shares a personal invite link.
- Friend signs up and completes their first week → **both unlock [reward placeholder: e.g. 1 free month / a premium program]**.
- Light gamification: a "crew" streak that grows as invited friends keep training.

**Why double-sided:** rewarding only the referrer feels transactional; rewarding both makes the invite feel like a gift, which converts better and fits the "together" message.

**Channels:**
1. In-app prompt at a high moment (right after a completed workout / streak milestone).
2. Lifecycle email + push to active users.
3. Shareable post-workout result card for Instagram/WhatsApp.

**Measure:** invites sent → friend signups → *friend week-1 activation* (the metric that matters), and referred-user retention vs. organic. Watch activation, not raw invite counts (a vanity number).
