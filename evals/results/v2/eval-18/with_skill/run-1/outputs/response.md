| Brand | Positioning | Target | Strength | Weakness / gap |

| **Purple Carrot** | Plant-based / vegan | Vegans & plant-curious | Genuinely veg-forward | Fully vegan (excludes dairy/egg vegetarians); smaller variety |

## The gap for a vegetarian-only kit
The big three treat vegetarian as a filter on a meat-first menu; Purple Carrot owns plant-based but is strictly **vegan**. That leaves an underserved middle: **lacto-ovo vegetarians** who want creative, protein-rich veg meals using dairy and eggs — not a watered-down "veggie option," and not full vegan restriction. A kit built *entirely* around satisfying, varied vegetarian cooking (with cheese/egg, global flavors, real protein) has a defensible identity none of them fully own.
