A positioning statement is the foundation everything else inherits — get it wrong and the whole site, deck, and ad set drift with it. So before I write one (and hand you something that looks authoritative but might be built on a guess), three sharp questions:

1. **Who exactly is it for?** The narrowest core user — e.g. PMs at Series-B consumer apps, growth engineers, or solo founders — not just "product teams."
2. **What's the one thing your analytics tool does that the main alternatives don't?** The single differentiator vs. the obvious incumbents.
3. **What are you really competing against** — Amplitude/Mixpanel, a GA4 setup, or teams flying blind with no analytics at all?

Give me those three and I'll write a tight positioning statement (plus the for/who/unlike structure). Prefer I just draft one on stated assumptions you can correct? Say the word.
